---
title: Upgrade Guide
description: Upgrade Nest Graph Inspector when the viewer asks for a newer graph output version.
navigation:
  icon: i-lucide-arrow-up-circle
---

- The website viewer expects `nest-graph-inspector` to stay on a current release, so upgrading is required for continued use.
- **Most updates are intended to be non-breaking**; when a breaking change is introduced, the major version is incremented.
- The project is currently in a pivot phase, so updates may be shipped more frequently than usual.

::package-manager-command{command="update"}
::
